# Notice

This is a public-review candidate export of a paper-card generation workflow and selected Markdown examples.

No paper PDFs, rendered page images, raw figure/table images, queue state, local logs, or broad workspace artifacts are intentionally included.

This repository is released under the MIT License. See `LICENSE`.

Example cards summarize research papers in Korean and may contain bibliographic facts, formulas, benchmark numbers, and paraphrased figure/table descriptions. The source papers retain their own copyright and license terms. Reviewers should verify that any public use of the summaries is allowed for the intended venue.

The included scripts are provided as utility examples. They require local PDF tools when checking a source paper or rendering pages.
